Total Lawn and Landscape

Total Lawn and Landscape specializes in wooden fencing and pride ourselves in our ability to create the privacy for your home or property with our expertise and knowledge. We have assisted many customers with various projects to beautify their existing home or property in the Birmingham, Alabama area. We also provide landscaping services including tree and shrub planting, mulch, and scheduled lawn care service.
